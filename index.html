What is ManyChat?

ManyChat is the market-leader in **Conversational Marketing Platforms (Chatbot Automation)**. It focuses on drag-and-drop conversational flows, trigger words, commenting campaigns, and social DMs (Instagram, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, SMS).

App Classification

This class of apps is called **Chatbot Builders / Messaging Automation Software**. They specialize in visual conditional workflows, lead capturing, and automated sales conversations within native instant messenger systems.

Our Integrated Value

Message Manager integrates the visual ease of **ManyChat**, the visitor identification/identity enrichment of **Customers.ai**, the shared WhatsApp team inbox of **WATI**, and the e-commerce customer support AI bots of **Tidio** into a single dashboard.
